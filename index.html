I <3 Quilting

Hello and welcome to "I < 3 Quilting". I am an avid crafter. I do various crafts: cross stitch, crochet and latchhook, among other. Quilting is a favorite hobby of mine. I made my first quilt top when I was 6... for one of my dolls. I love being creative and using my skills for friends and family. This website will provide some tools and information for crafters.
